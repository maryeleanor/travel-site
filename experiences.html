Holidays in the French Countryside

I never imagined I would celebrate Christmas in France, nevermind at a farmhhouse with over 20 of my extended relatives. Christmas mass at the famed Chartres Cathedral, followed by a walk through the garden labyrinth. We enjoyed homemade duck confit, french onion soup, roasted quails and endless cheese and bread.

Trying Pulque in Mexcio City

We all love finding old-school dive bars, but this place was like no other. A punk rock bar hidden in an alley way serving just one drink, that you can't get anywhere else. Pulque is a thick, fermented juice made from the sap of the agave plant. The flavor is like as a mix between a fruit juice and kombucha, but thick like aloe or milk, with an alcoholic content of strong beer.

Ice Climbing on a Glacier

Learn what it was like dropping into a black hole on an ice covered mountain in the Iceland highlands, knowing it was up to you and your personal strength to claw yourself out. I've been rock climbing and bouldering, but this was so much more intense. We started with a belay down a crevace, and got to really learn how to use those cramp-ons and axes.
